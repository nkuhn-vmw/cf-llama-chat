# CF Llama Chat — Settings and Personalization

> Customize your AI experience with themes, default models, system instructions, language preferences, and data management controls.

---

## Problem Statement

Every user works differently. Some prefer dark mode for late-night coding sessions. Others need responses in Spanish or want every conversation to start with a specific system instruction. A one-size-fits-all AI interface doesn't serve diverse teams well. CF Llama Chat provides deep personalization so every user gets an experience tailored to how they work.

---

## Scene 1: Accessing Settings

Click your avatar or username in the top-right header area to open the user menu. From the dropdown, you'll see your display name, role, and quick links to My Workspace, Usage Metrics, Admin Portal (if you're an admin), and Settings. Click "Settings" to open the full settings page.

---

## Scene 2: Appearance Customization

The Appearance section controls how the entire interface looks and feels.

### Theme selection

Choose between three themes. Dark mode provides a deep background ideal for extended use and low-light environments. Light mode offers a bright, clean interface for well-lit workspaces. OLED mode uses a true black background optimized for OLED displays — perfect for saving battery on mobile devices or achieving maximum contrast.

### Background style

Select from three background options. "None" gives you a clean, solid background. "Gradient" adds a subtle color gradient for a modern aesthetic. "Pattern" applies a textured pattern to the background.

### Font size

Adjust the interface font size between Small, Medium, and Large to match your visual preferences and display resolution.

### Message density

Control how much space appears between chat messages. "Compact" fits more messages on screen. "Normal" provides a balanced layout. "Comfortable" adds extra spacing for a more relaxed reading experience.

---

## Scene 3: Chat Preferences

The Chat Preferences section controls default behavior for all your conversations.

### Default model

Select your preferred AI model from the dropdown. Every new conversation will start with this model pre-selected, saving you from switching each time. You can always override the model for individual conversations.

### Streaming responses

Toggle streaming on or off by default. When enabled, AI responses appear token by token in real time. When disabled, the complete response loads at once. This setting applies to new conversations — you can override it per conversation using the Stream toggle in the chat input area.

### Auto-title conversations

Enable this to have the AI automatically generate descriptive titles for your conversations based on the content of your first message. This saves you from manually naming every chat and makes your conversation list more searchable.

### Custom system instructions

Write up to 2000 characters of system instructions that are prepended to every conversation. These instructions shape how the AI responds to you across all chats. For example: "Always respond in bullet points and include code examples in Python" or "You are a senior Java developer helping me with Spring Boot applications. Be concise and opinionated." This is a powerful way to consistently tailor the AI to your specific role and preferences.

---

## Scene 4: Language, Region, and Notifications

### Language selection

Choose your preferred interface language from English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, or Arabic. The interface adapts to your selection.

### Date format

Select your preferred date format — MM/DD/YYYY for US convention, DD/MM/YYYY for European convention, or YYYY-MM-DD for ISO standard.

### Desktop notifications

Enable desktop notifications to receive browser alerts for new channel messages and other platform events — even when the CF Llama Chat tab is in the background.

### Sound notifications

Toggle sound notifications on or off for an audible alert when new messages arrive.

---

## Scene 5: Data Management and Privacy

The Data and Privacy section gives you full control over your personal data.

### Export your data

Click "Export All Data" to download a complete JSON file containing all your conversations, messages, notes, memories, and settings. This is useful for backup, compliance audits, or migrating to another platform.

### Delete all conversations

Click "Delete All Conversations" to remove your entire chat history. A confirmation dialog ensures you don't accidentally delete everything. This action is irreversible.

### Delete your account

In the Danger Zone section, click "Delete Account" to permanently remove your account and all associated data from the platform. This requires password confirmation and cannot be undone. Use this when offboarding from the platform entirely.

---

## Summary

CF Llama Chat's settings give every user full control over their experience. Choose from Dark, Light, or OLED themes. Set your default model and streaming preferences. Write persistent system instructions that shape every conversation. Select your language and date format. Manage your data with export, bulk delete, and account deletion options. Every setting is designed to make the AI work the way you work.
